Why Choose GCT Hilal School?

GCT Hilal School Mithi is committed to providing quality education in a nurturing and disciplined environment. Our experienced faculty ensures each student reaches their full potential.

  • Qualified and experienced teaching staff
  • Classes from Nursery to Matric (Class 10)
  • Modern classrooms and learning resources
  • Separate sections for boys and girls
  • Annual sports and co-curricular activities
  • Affordable and transparent fee structure
  • Regular parent-teacher meetings
  • Scholarship for top-performing students
